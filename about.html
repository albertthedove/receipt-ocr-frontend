Who created this tool?

This is a small, independent side project built for people in South Africa who deal with long paper receipts every day — especially from supermarkets like Pick n Pay, Checkers, Shoprite, Woolworths, Clicks, Dis-Chem, and smaller stores.

I got tired of manually typing out grocery lists or expense items from receipts, so I decided to build something that uses modern OCR + AI to do the heavy lifting from a quick video instead of a photo.

Why video instead of just a photo?

  • Many people already record short videos of receipts (e.g. proof of purchase, warranty, returns)
  • Video often captures more context and better angles in one take
  • It can help when parts of the receipt are slightly curved or reflective

Current features (2026)

  • Extracts item names, quantities, unit prices, line totals
  • Detects barcodes (EAN-13, UPC, etc.) when clearly visible
  • Shows grand total in South African Rand (R)
  • One-click CSV download for Excel, Google Sheets, etc.
  • Warm-up button to reduce cold-start delay on first use
