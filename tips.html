Video recording tips

  1. Use bright, even lighting — avoid direct flash or strong shadows
  2. Hold the receipt as flat as possible
  3. Move slowly and steadily across the entire receipt (top to bottom)
  4. Keep the camera 20–40 cm away so text is sharp
  5. Record in landscape if the receipt is wide
  6. Make sure there is no glare/reflection on shiny thermal paper

Improving barcode detection

  • Slow down when passing over the barcode area
  • Make sure the barcode is not folded or creased
  • Good lighting is especially important for barcode reading

After processing

  • Check the total amount matches your memory/receipt
  • Verify any high-value items manually
  • Use the CSV in Google Sheets or Excel for further analysis or budgeting

Still not good results?

Try recording again with better lighting or a steadier hand. Very long till slips (>1 meter) may need to be recorded in sections.
