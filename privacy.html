What we collect and why

We only process the video file you explicitly upload for the purpose of extracting receipt information. We do not collect any personal identifiers from the receipt content (names, ID numbers, card details, etc. are not intentionally extracted or stored).

Data retention

  • Uploaded videos are processed in memory / temporarily on our server
  • Videos and extracted results are automatically deleted within minutes after processing completes
  • No permanent copy of your video or full extracted data is kept

What we do NOT do

  • Sell your data
  • Use your receipts to train public AI models
  • Share identifiable data with third parties for marketing
