Why does the first process take so long?

The server uses a "serverless" setup (Google Cloud Run). The first request after inactivity causes a cold start which can take 60–180 seconds. Use the "Warm Up API" button first to avoid this delay.

Which stores/receipts work best?

Most major South African chains (Pick n Pay, Checkers, Shoprite, Woolworths, Clicks, Dis-Chem, Spar, Boxer) work reasonably well. Thermal receipts and very small font can be more challenging.

Can it read handwritten receipts?

Handwritten text is currently not reliably supported. The tool works best with printed retail receipts.

Does it extract every single line?

It tries to, but may miss or misread lines if the video quality is poor, lighting is bad, or text is very small/blurry. Barcodes are only extracted when clearly visible.

Is my data safe?

Videos are processed temporarily and deleted shortly after. We do not store your receipts or use them for training. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use this for business / many receipts?

Yes — but keep in mind this is currently a free hobby project with no guaranteed uptime or bulk processing support.
